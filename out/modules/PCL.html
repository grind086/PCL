PCL Module

Defined in: src/PCL.js:49

Node-based procedural noise library with utilities for turning that noise into useful data structures, such as textures. Based on the C++ library libnoise by Jason Bevins (http://libnoise.sourceforge.net/index.html).

Nodes are broken down into four different categories: Generators, Modifiers, Combiners, and Selectors.

Generators
Generators have no inputs, and one output. They provide the base values for the rest of the node chain.

  • ArgumentNode
  • ConstantNode

Modifiers
Modifiers have one input, and one output.

  • AbsNode
  • ClampNode
  • CurveNode
  • InvertNode
  • ScaleBiasNode
  • TerraceNode

Combiners
Combiners have two inputs, and one output.

  • AddNode
  • DivideNode
  • MaxNode
  • MinNode
  • MultiplyNode
  • PowerNode
  • SubtractNode

Selectors
Selectors have three inputs, and one output.

  • BlendNode
  • SelectNode
